Events

Stay informed about upcoming events related to political engagement and voter rights.

Political events offer young adults a valuable chance to engage directly with important issues and gain insight into the workings of their communities and the nation. Attending rallies, town halls, debates, and campaign events provides the opportunity to hear candidates speak firsthand, understand their policy positions, and even pose questions about issues that matter most to you. These gatherings foster dynamic conversations that can shed light on the goals and values of different leaders and parties. They also connect you with peers who share your passions, helping you build a network of like-minded individuals who are equally dedicated to making a difference.


Being engaged in politics means more than just voting; it involves staying informed, volunteering, advocating for causes you believe in, and supporting organizations that drive change. For young adults, even simple actions like attending a local council meeting or participating in a phone bank can help drive significant change. Regularly engaging with political issues keeps you connected to the shifting world around you and equips you with the knowledge and tools needed to be an active, informed voice in democracy.
